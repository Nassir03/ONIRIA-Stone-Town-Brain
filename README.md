# ONIRIA Stone Town Brain

A connected Obsidian knowledge and operating system for ONIRIA Stone Town, a 20-key AI-native luxury boutique hotel in Stone Town, Zanzibar.

## Open first

- `00 Control/ONIRIA Brain Dashboard.md`
- `01 REFERENCEs/REFERENCE Index.md`

## Completed scope in this package

- **Property and rooms:** Property, floors, room categories, Rooms 01-20, public areas, building systems and room inspection.
- **Operations and SOPs:** Connected priority SOPs, service standards and daily/opening/closing checklists.
- **Maintenance and inventory:** Equipment, maintenance, inspections, warranties, suppliers, inventory, spares and reorder controls.
- **People, safety and compliance:** Departments, staff roles, onboarding, training, duty structure, safety, privacy and compliance.
