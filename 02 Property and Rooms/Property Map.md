## Property Identity

ONIRIA Stone Town is a 20-key luxury boutique hotel in Stone Town, Zanzibar, Tanzania, inside a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

## Operating Shape

The hotel is structured as:

- Ground floor: reception, 5 guest rooms, spa
- First floor: 8 guest rooms
- Second floor: 7 guest rooms
- Rooftop: infinity pool, bar, lounge

F&B is connected to SHI, the building next door, which supports breakfast, lunch, dinner, and rooftop service.
