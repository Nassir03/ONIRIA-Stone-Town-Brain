# Room Inspection Structure

## Purpose

This structure gives every room a consistent inspection record without duplicating maintenance schedules or housekeeping procedures.

## Inspection Types

| Inspection Type | Trigger | Owner | Related Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-opening room readiness | Before opening | Operations lead | [[Opening and Closing Checklists]] |
| Arrival readiness | Before guest arrival | Housekeeping / Front office | [[Guest Journey Overview]] |
| Daily stayover check | During occupancy | Housekeeping | [[Housekeeping SOPs]] |
| Turndown check | Evening service | Housekeeping | [[Turndown Service SOP]] |
| Maintenance issue check | When defect reported | Engineering | [[Maintenance Request SOP]] |
| Post-checkout inspection | After departure | Housekeeping / Maintenance | [[Check-Out Procedure]] |
| Safety inspection | Scheduled or incident-based | Safety lead | [[Emergency SOPs]] |

## Required Inspection Fields

- Inspection ID
- Room
- Date and time
- Inspector
- Occupancy status
- Cleanliness status
- Guest-impacting issues
- Maintenance issues
- Safety issues
- Equipment affected
- Photo or evidence link
- Corrective action
- Assigned owner
- Deadline
- Status
- Related maintenance request
- Related incident
- Source

## Room-Level Standards

Each room note must show:

- Room number
- Category
- Floor
- Features
- Furniture and equipment summary
- Lighting
- HVAC
- KNX
- Wi-Fi
- Speakers
- Sensors
- Smart lock
- Cleaning standard
- Maintenance information
- Related SOPs
