# Equipment Register

## Purpose

One record per maintainable asset. Do not use this register for loose room design lists unless the item needs maintenance, warranty, safety, stock or lifecycle tracking.

## Required asset fields

- Asset ID and asset class
- Exact location: property/floor/room/zone
- Building system
- Manufacturer/model/serial number
- Supplier and purchase record
- Install/commission date
- Warranty dates and terms
- Manual/REFERENCE
- Safety and isolation information
- Criticality and guest impact
- Preventive-maintenance plan
- Spare parts and reorder links
- Current condition/status
- Open requests/incidents

## Initial asset classes

- HVAC units and controllers
- KNX panels, actuators and gateways
- Lighting control equipment
- Network switches, access points and gateways
- Speakers/amplifiers/voice interfaces
- Smart locks and access-control devices
- Pumps, water heaters and plumbing equipment
- Pool plant and treatment equipment
- Spa equipment
- Kitchen/F&B interface equipment where hotel-owned
- Fire/life-safety equipment
- Laundry/housekeeping equipment

## Current population status

Model numbers, quantities and installed locations are TO CONFIRM from approved schedules, submittals, purchase orders and commissioning records.
