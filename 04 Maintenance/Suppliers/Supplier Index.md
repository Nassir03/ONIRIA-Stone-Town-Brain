# Supplier Index

## Known current supplier/partner

- [[Supplier - Elements Concept]]

## Qualification before contact

- Product fit and quality tier
- Exact catalog/model/specification
- Certifications and warranty
- Lead time and minimum quantity
- Zanzibar shipping capability and Incoterms
- Installation/commissioning support
- Price and total landed-cost evidence
- References and delivery performance

## Communication rule

Research first. Contact only qualified suppliers. Ask only for unavailable facts, exact quotation, quantity/date availability or clarification. Keep communication in writing; do not propose supplier calls.
