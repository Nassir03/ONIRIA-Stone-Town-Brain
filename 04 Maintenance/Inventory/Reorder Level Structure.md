# Reorder Level Structure

## Formula

**Reorder point = expected demand during replenishment lead time + safety stock.**

Use verified consumption, confirmed supplier lead time, delivery variability, shelf life, storage capacity, criticality and Zanzibar logistics. Do not set arbitrary levels.

## Decision classes

- **A - Critical:** Service or safety stops without item; high safety stock and daily monitoring.
- **B - Important:** Noticeable service impact; weekly monitoring.
- **C - Routine:** Easily substituted or short lead time; normal cycle count.

## Required approval

Item owner proposes; department head verifies usage; finance/procurement approves commercial parameters.
