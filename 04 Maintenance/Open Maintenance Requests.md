## Priority definition

- **Emergency:** Immediate threat to life, fire, flood, electrical hazard, structural risk or major security risk.
- **Urgent:** Occupied-room failure or major service interruption requiring rapid response.
- **Routine:** Limited impact with safe workaround.
- **Planned:** Improvement or non-urgent corrective work scheduled with access and parts.
