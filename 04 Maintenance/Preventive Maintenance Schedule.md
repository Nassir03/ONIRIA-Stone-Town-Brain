# Preventive Maintenance Schedule

## Scheduling method

Create maintenance frequencies only from approved manufacturer manuals, warranty conditions, risk assessment, statutory/authority requirements and operating experience. Do not invent intervals.

| Asset | Location | Task source | Frequency | Last done | Next due | Owner | Guest impact | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| TO CONFIRM |  | Manual/approved plan | TO CONFIRM |  |  |  |  | Open |

## Priority rules

- Life-safety and water-quality tasks cannot be deferred without competent approval.
- Schedule noisy, disruptive or service-affecting work around occupancy.
- Link every completed task to evidence and asset history.
- Repeated corrective repairs trigger root-cause review.
