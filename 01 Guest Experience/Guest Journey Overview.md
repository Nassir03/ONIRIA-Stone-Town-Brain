# Guest Journey Overview

## Purpose

This note describes the complete guest journey for ONIRIA Stone Town Hotel, from the first reservation request until post-stay follow-up.

The journey connects front office, reservations, rooms, housekeeping, food and beverage, spa, maintenance, guest requests, complaints, privacy, staff responsibilities, and Lulu AI.
