# Preference Confidence Levels

Use:

- High: directly confirmed by the guest
- Medium: recorded by authorised staff but not recently reconfirmed
- Low: old, incomplete, or indirect information
- Unverified: not confirmed by the guest or authorised REFERENCE

# Staff Rules

Staff must:

- Record only relevant information
- Confirm important preferences
- Avoid assumptions
- Protect private information
- Share only the minimum necessary information
- Update outdated preferences
- Record the REFERENCE
- Respect guest consent
- Remove or archive information according to policy

Staff must not:

- Record insulting or unnecessary comments
- Guess sensitive information
- Share preferences in public channels
- use restricted information for unrelated purposes
- retain information without an approved reason

# Lulu AI Support

Lulu may:

- Retrieve authorised guest preferences
- Identify missing profile information
- Prepare pre-arrival checklists
- Notify relevant departments
- Detect conflicting preferences
- Suggest personalised service based on verified information
- Track active guest requests
- Prepare approved messages
- Flag outdated information

Lulu must not:

- Reveal restricted guest data
- Invent preferences
- use unverified information for important actions
- contact the guest through an unapproved channel
- change consent records without human review
