# Guest Request and Complaint Register Templates

## Purpose

This note provides standard templates for recording, assigning, tracking, escalating, completing, and reviewing guest requests and complaints.

The templates should help the hotel ensure that:

- Every request is recorded
- Every complaint has an owner
- Deadlines are visible
- Approvals are recorded
- Completion evidence is available
- Guest satisfaction is checked
- Serious issues are escalated
- Final closure is traceable

# Privacy Rules

Guest request and complaint records must:

- Be accessible only to authorised users
- Use guest references instead of unnecessary personal details
- Avoid including sensitive data in public channels
- Protect payment, identification, health, and security information
- Record access where required
- Follow approved retention rules
- Share only the minimum necessary information

# Lulu AI Support

Lulu may:

- Create request and complaint records
- Classify categories
- Suggest priority
- Assign approved routine tasks
- Track deadlines
- Detect overdue actions
- Prepare reminders
- Request missing information
- Prepare service-recovery recommendations
- Request human approval
- Prepare daily summaries
- Connect complaints to tasks, risks, decisions, SOPs, and training
- Flag repeated service failures

Lulu must not:

- Close unresolved complaints
- Offer compensation independently
- Remove charges
- Share restricted guest information
- Mark completion without evidence
- Hide delays
- change approval records
- classify a serious safety or privacy complaint as routine
