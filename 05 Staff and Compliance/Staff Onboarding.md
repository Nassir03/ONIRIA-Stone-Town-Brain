# Staff Onboarding

## Purpose

Onboarding gives each team member the minimum knowledge needed to operate safely, protect guests, and use the ONIRIA Brain.

## Onboarding Path

1. Brand, guest promise, and ONIRIA service tone
2. Property orientation
3. Department role and responsibilities
4. Guest privacy and data access
5. Safety and emergency basics
6. Role-specific SOPs
7. Lulu AI and Brain use
8. Training confirmation

## Required Records

- Staff member
- Role
- Department
- Start date
- Required training
- Completed training
- Trainer
- Evidence
- Access permissions
- Sign-off status
