# Staff Training Matrix

## Purpose

The training matrix connects each role to required training, evidence, refresh dates, and safety-critical approvals.

## Training Areas

| Training | Required For | Refresh | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Guest privacy | All staff | Annual | Signed record |
| Fire safety | All staff | Annual / as required | Drill or certificate |
| Emergency response | All staff | Annual | Drill record |
| Food allergy handling | F&B, spa, front office | Annual | Training record |
| Pool safety | Rooftop, F&B, security, operations | Annual | Training record |
| Room inspection | Housekeeping, engineering | Quarterly | Supervisor sign-off |
| Maintenance reporting | All operational staff | Annual | Practice record |
| Lulu and Brain use | Staff with system access | As updated | Access and test evidence |

## Training Record Fields

- Staff member
- Role
- Department
- Training name
- Required level
- Completed date
- Expiry date
- Trainer
- Evidence link
- Status
