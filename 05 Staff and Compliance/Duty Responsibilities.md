# Duty Responsibilities

## Purpose

This note defines what must be covered during every operating shift.

## Duty Coverage

- Duty manager
- Front office contact
- Housekeeping supervisor
- Engineering contact
- Security contact
- F&B or rooftop contact
- Spa contact when operating
- Emergency lead

## Duty Handover Fields

- Date and shift
- Duty manager
- Arrivals
- Departures
- VIPs or special requests
- Rooms out of order
- Open maintenance issues
- Open complaints
- Safety concerns
- Stock concerns
- Staff shortages
- Pending approvals
- Follow-up deadlines
