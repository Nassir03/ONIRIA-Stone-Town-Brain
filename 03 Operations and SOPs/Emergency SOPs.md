# Emergency SOPs

## Purpose

Emergency SOPs provide immediate operating guidance for life safety, security, medical, fire, equipment, and severe guest incidents.

## Emergency Types

- Fire
- Medical emergency
- Injury
- Security threat
- Flood or major leak
- Electrical hazard
- Food allergy reaction
- Pool incident
- Privacy breach
- Missing person or child

## Immediate Response

1. Protect life and safety.
2. Stop unsafe activity when safe to do so.
3. Notify responsible emergency role.
4. Contact emergency services according to approved local procedure.
5. Preserve evidence.
6. Record time, place, people involved, and actions taken.
7. Communicate clearly to guests and staff.
8. Close the incident only after authorised review.
