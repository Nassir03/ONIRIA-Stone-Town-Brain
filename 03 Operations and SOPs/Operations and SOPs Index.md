## Required SOP Metadata

Every SOP should include:

- Department
- Responsible staff role
- Related room or area
- Guest journey stage
- Guest request type
- Equipment where applicable
- Safety rule
- Incident type
- Escalation path
- Lulu workflow
- Approved REFERENCE

## Do Not Duplicate

This section should not recreate full department structures, staff job descriptions, equipment inventory, supplier records, guest profiles, building maps, or Lulu permission rules.
