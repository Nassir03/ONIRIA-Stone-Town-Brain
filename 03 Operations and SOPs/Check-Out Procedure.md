# Check-Out Procedure

## Purpose

Complete departure accurately, protect guest privacy, capture feedback, close billing, and return the room to operational control.

## Required Inputs

- Guest account
- Booking record
- Pending charges
- Payment status
- Room number
- Transport requirement
- Complaint or feedback status
- Lost-property status where relevant

## Procedure

1. Confirm guest name and room.
2. Review account and pending charges.
3. Process payment according to approved policy.
4. Ask whether any issue remains unresolved.
5. Record feedback or complaint.
6. Confirm transport or luggage support.
7. Collect access items where applicable.
8. Update room status for housekeeping.
9. Trigger post-checkout inspection.
10. Send receipt and farewell message.

## Escalation

Escalate when there is:

- Charge dispute
- Refund request
- Compensation request
- Damage claim
- Lost property
- Privacy complaint
- Serious service failure
