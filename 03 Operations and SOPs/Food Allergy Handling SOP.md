# Food Allergy Handling SOP

## Purpose

Prevent allergy harm by recording, confirming, communicating, and verifying allergy information before food or drink service.

## Procedure

1. Ask the guest to state allergies or dietary restrictions.
2. Record only necessary information in the approved guest profile or booking record.
3. Confirm the allergen clearly with the guest.
4. Notify F&B, spa, and front office when the allergy affects service.
5. Check menu item ingredients and cross-contact risk.
6. Do not serve uncertain items.
7. Escalate severe allergy risk to the duty manager and F&B lead.
8. Record incident or near miss if a mistake occurs.

## Critical Rule

When ingredient or cross-contact information is unknown, the answer is not approved for service.
