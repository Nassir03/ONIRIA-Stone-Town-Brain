# Lost and Found SOP

## Purpose

Protect guest property, privacy, and evidence when an item is found, reported missing, stored, returned, or disposed of.

## Procedure

1. Record item description, location found, date, time, finder, and room or area.
2. Photograph item where appropriate without exposing sensitive contents.
3. Store securely.
4. Notify front office.
5. Contact guest only through approved channel when permitted.
6. Verify ownership before release.
7. Record release, shipping, disposal, or unresolved status.

## Privacy Rule

Do not inspect private contents unless required for safety or approved procedure.
