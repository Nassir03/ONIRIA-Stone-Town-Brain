## Purpose

Front office SOPs control reservation support, arrival, check-in, guest requests, complaints, check-out, lost property, and post-stay follow-up.

## Core Service Standard

Front office must be calm, exact, privacy-aware, and fast. Guests should not have to repeat information already captured in the Brain, PMS, or approved guest preference workflow.
