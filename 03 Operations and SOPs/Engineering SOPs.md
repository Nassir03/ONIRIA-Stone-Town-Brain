# Engineering SOPs

## Purpose

Engineering SOPs keep building systems safe, reliable, guest-invisible, and documented.

## Operating Areas

- Electrical
- Plumbing
- HVAC
- KNX and controls
- Wi-Fi and low voltage
- Smart locks
- Speakers
- Pools
- Spa systems
- Fire and life safety interfaces

## Core Standards

- Guest safety comes first.
- Guest comfort issues are logged with room revenue impact.
- Hidden infrastructure must be documented before closure.
- Temporary fixes must be marked temporary.
- Critical systems require evidence before closure.
