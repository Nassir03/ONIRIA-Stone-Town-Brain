# Spa Booking SOP

## Purpose

Book spa services accurately while protecting guest preferences, health information, and therapist availability.

## Procedure

1. Receive request from guest, front office, web, WhatsApp, or Lulu.
2. Confirm guest name, room or booking reference, preferred time, treatment, number of guests, and privacy permissions.
3. Check therapist and room availability.
4. Ask only necessary health, allergy, pregnancy, injury, or sensitivity questions.
5. Confirm price and cancellation rule when approved.
6. Record booking.
7. Notify spa team.
8. Confirm appointment to guest.
9. Record completion, no-show, cancellation, or follow-up.

## Escalation

Escalate medical uncertainty, privacy concerns, harassment, injury, or compensation requests.
