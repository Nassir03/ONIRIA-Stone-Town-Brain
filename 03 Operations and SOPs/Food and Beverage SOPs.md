# Food and Beverage SOPs

## Purpose

F&B SOPs connect ONIRIA and SHI service, guest preferences, allergies, rooftop operations, stock, suppliers, and safety.

## Connected SOPs

- [[Food Allergy Handling SOP]]
- [[Guest Privacy SOP]]
- [[Emergency SOPs]]
- [[Opening and Closing Checklists]]

## Core Standards

- Confirm allergies before service.
- Keep guest preferences private and operationally useful.
- Record stock movement for high-value or controlled items.
- Escalate food safety, illness, intoxication, and injury concerns.
- Coordinate rooftop service with pool safety.
