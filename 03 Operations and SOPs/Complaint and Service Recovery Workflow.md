# Complaint and Service Recovery Workflow

## Purpose

Resolve guest dissatisfaction respectfully, quickly, and consistently while protecting safety, privacy, money, and reputation.

## Procedure

1. Listen and acknowledge the guest's experience.
2. Record the complaint with date, time, guest reference, room, issue, and evidence.
3. Classify severity: low, medium, high, critical.
4. Identify responsible department.
5. Check related room, equipment, staff duty, or SOP.
6. Prepare a recovery action.
7. Request approval for compensation, refund, upgrade, or external response.
8. Communicate the approved action.
9. Track completion.
10. Confirm guest satisfaction.
11. Record root cause and prevention action.

## Immediate Escalation

Escalate immediately for:

- Injury
- Fire or life safety
- Security threat
- Privacy breach
- Discrimination or harassment
- Food allergy or illness
- Repeated unresolved failure
- Major financial exposure
