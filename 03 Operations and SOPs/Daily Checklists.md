# Daily Checklists

## Purpose

Daily checklists keep repeated hotel work visible without replacing SOPs.

## Morning

- Review arrivals, departures, stayovers, VIPs, preferences, allergies, and special requests.
- Confirm room readiness priorities.
- Check open maintenance issues.
- Review safety and security notes.
- Check stock exceptions.
- Confirm staff coverage.

## Midday

- Review room status.
- Check guest requests and deadlines.
- Confirm spa and F&B bookings.
- Update unresolved complaints.
- Review supplier or delivery issues.

## Evening

- Confirm turndown priorities.
- Check late arrivals.
- Review rooftop, bar, pool, and security status.
- Confirm open incidents or risks.
- Prepare handover.
