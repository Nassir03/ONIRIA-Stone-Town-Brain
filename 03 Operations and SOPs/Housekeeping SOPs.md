# Housekeeping SOPs

## Purpose

Housekeeping SOPs maintain cleanliness, room readiness, guest comfort, privacy, and defect reporting.

## Scope

- Pre-arrival room preparation
- Stayover cleaning
- Post-checkout cleaning
- Turndown service
- Linen and amenities
- Lost property discovery
- Defect reporting
- Privacy protection

## Room Readiness Procedure

1. Confirm room assignment and arrival deadline.
2. Clean room according to approved standard.
3. Check bathroom, bed, minibar, amenities, lighting, AC, Wi-Fi, speakers, lock, and visible damage.
4. Confirm guest preferences where permitted.
5. Report defects through [[Maintenance Request SOP]].
6. Mark room status as ready only when cleaning and inspection are complete.
