# Check-In Procedure

## Purpose

Complete guest registration, confirm identity and booking details, issue room access, and begin the stay with a calm ONIRIA welcome.

## Required Inputs

- Reservation record
- Guest identity requirement
- Payment or guarantee status
- Room assignment
- Room readiness status
- Guest preferences
- Arrival notes
- Privacy permissions

## Procedure

1. Welcome the guest by name when known.
2. Confirm reservation and identity using approved policy.
3. Confirm arrival and departure dates.
4. Confirm room number and category.
5. Confirm payment method or guarantee.
6. Review special requests, allergies, accessibility needs, and privacy preferences.
7. Confirm the room is ready through housekeeping status.
8. Issue approved room access.
9. Explain essential services: Wi-Fi, breakfast/F&B, spa, safety, front-office contact, and privacy.
10. Escort or direct the guest to the room.
11. Mark check-in complete in the PMS and Brain where approved.

## Escalation

Escalate to the duty manager when there is:

- Unconfirmed booking
- Payment exception
- Identity issue
- Room not ready
- Guest dissatisfaction
- Safety or privacy concern
- Upgrade, refund, or compensation request
