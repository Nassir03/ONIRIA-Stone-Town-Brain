# Turndown Service SOP

## Purpose

Prepare the room for evening rest while preserving privacy, calm, and guest preference.

## Procedure

1. Check occupancy and do-not-disturb status.
2. Confirm approved service window.
3. Enter only when permitted.
4. Refresh towels, water, amenities, and visible room comfort items.
5. Set approved lighting, scent, music, and climate preferences where recorded.
6. Check visible defects and report them.
7. Respect personal belongings and guest data.
8. Record completion.
