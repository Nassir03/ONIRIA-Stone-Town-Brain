# Opening and Closing Checklists

## Purpose

Opening and closing checklists prepare daily operating areas and support the pre-opening path toward hotel launch.

## Daily Opening

- Confirm duty manager and department coverage.
- Check safety and security status.
- Confirm reception readiness.
- Confirm guest-facing areas are clean.
- Confirm rooms due for arrival are inspected.
- Check open maintenance issues.
- Confirm F&B, spa, and rooftop readiness.
- Check critical stock.

## Daily Closing

- Confirm all guest requests are closed or handed over.
- Confirm cash, payment, and PMS close procedures when approved.
- Confirm security check.
- Confirm rooftop and pool close.
- Confirm spa close.
- Confirm unresolved issues are recorded.
- Prepare handover for next shift.

## Pre-Opening Readiness

- Complete room-by-room inspection.
- Verify building systems.
- Confirm SOP approval.
- Train staff on safety and privacy.
- Confirm supplier and inventory records.
- Complete compliance register.
