# Spa SOPs

## Purpose

Spa SOPs protect guest privacy, treatment quality, hygiene, scheduling, and service recovery.

## Operating Standards

- Confirm booking, treatment, therapist, time, guest needs, contraindications, and privacy permissions.
- Keep treatment records private.
- Check product allergies and sensitivities.
- Maintain hygiene and room readiness.
- Escalate medical, privacy, or safety concerns.
