# Maintenance Request SOP

## Purpose

Record, classify, assign, complete, and verify maintenance issues without losing guest impact or safety context.

## Procedure

1. Receive issue from guest, staff, inspection, sensor, or Lulu.
2. Record room or area, equipment, issue, severity, guest impact, and evidence.
3. Check whether the room or area must be taken out of service.
4. Assign responsible technician or supplier.
5. Set deadline based on urgency.
6. Communicate status to front office when guest-facing.
7. Complete repair or safe temporary action.
8. Record parts used and warranty relevance.
9. Verify result through inspection.
10. Close request only with evidence.

## Severity

| Severity | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | Life safety, fire, electrical hazard, flood, security lock failure | Immediate escalation |
| High | Guest room unsellable or major comfort failure | Same-day response |
| Medium | Service issue with workaround | Scheduled response |
| Low | Minor defect | Planned maintenance |
