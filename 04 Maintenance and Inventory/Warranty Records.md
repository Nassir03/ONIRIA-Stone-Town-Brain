# Warranty Records

## Purpose

Warranty records protect ONIRIA from avoidable repair cost and help track equipment accountability.

## Warranty Fields

- Warranty ID
- Equipment
- Supplier
- Manufacturer
- Purchase date
- Installation date
- Commissioning date
- Warranty start
- Warranty end
- Coverage
- Exclusions
- Claim process
- Required evidence
- Related manual
- Related maintenance requests
- Status

## Warranty Status

- Pending documentation
- Active
- Claim submitted
- Claim approved
- Claim rejected
- Expired
