# Reorder Levels

## Purpose

Reorder levels prevent guest-impacting shortages by connecting stock, supplier lead time, storage, and consumption rate.

## Reorder Fields

- Item
- Category
- Current stock
- Minimum stock
- Reorder point
- Reorder quantity
- Average daily or weekly use
- Supplier
- Lead time
- Criticality
- Guest impact if out of stock
- Safety impact if out of stock
- Last reviewed

## Criticality

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Critical | Safety, legal, or room sellability affected |
| High | Guest service noticeably affected |
| Medium | Operational convenience affected |
| Low | Non-urgent replacement |
