# Supplier Performance Records

## Purpose

Supplier performance records help ONIRIA keep only reliable operational suppliers.

## Performance Fields

- Supplier
- Product or service
- Order or service reference
- Delivery date promised
- Delivery date actual
- Quality result
- Documentation completeness
- Warranty support
- Communication quality
- Issue summary
- Resolution
- Rating
- Recommended status

## Rating Values

- Excellent
- Good
- Watch
- Poor
- Do not use
