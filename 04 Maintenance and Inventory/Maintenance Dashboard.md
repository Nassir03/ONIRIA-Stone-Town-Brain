# Maintenance Dashboard

## Purpose

This dashboard shows the operational health of rooms, equipment, suppliers, inventory, warranties, and open maintenance work.

## Views

| View | Purpose | Connected Note |
|---|---|---|
| Open maintenance requests | Track defects and response deadlines | [[Maintenance Request Template]] |
| Critical assets | Watch guest-impacting equipment | [[Equipment Inventory]] |
| Preventive maintenance | Track scheduled care | [[Preventive Maintenance Schedule]] |
| Inspections | Verify condition and closure | [[Inspection Records]] |
| Warranty risks | Avoid paying for covered defects | [[Warranty Records]] |
| Supplier issues | Track quality and delivery | [[Supplier Performance Records]] |
| Stock exceptions | Prevent operational shortages | [[Reorder Levels]] |

## Priority Rules

- Life safety overrides guest comfort.
- Guest-room sellability overrides routine maintenance.
- Water, electricity, locks, AC, pool systems, and fire safety are high-priority systems.
- Warranty-covered work should still be completed promptly, then reconciled.
