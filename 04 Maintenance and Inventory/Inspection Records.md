# Inspection Records

## Purpose

Inspection records verify room, equipment, safety, supplier, and maintenance outcomes.

## Inspection Register Fields

- Inspection ID
- Date and time
- Inspector
- Room or area
- Equipment
- Inspection type
- Checklist used
- Condition
- Defects found
- Guest impact
- Safety impact
- Evidence link
- Maintenance request
- Follow-up owner
- Deadline
- Status

## Inspection Types

- Pre-opening
- Daily operational
- Post-checkout
- Maintenance completion
- Safety
- Supplier delivery
- Warranty
- Incident follow-up
