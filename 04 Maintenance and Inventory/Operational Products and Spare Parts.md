# Operational Products and Spare Parts

## Purpose

This register tracks products and spare parts needed to operate, maintain, clean, repair, and serve the hotel.

## Categories

- HVAC filters and consumables
- Smart-lock batteries or parts
- Lighting drivers, lamps, and controls
- KNX parts
- Wi-Fi and network spares
- Pool chemicals and test kits
- Plumbing repair parts
- Spa products
- Cleaning products
- Linen and guest amenities
- F&B operating supplies
- Safety equipment consumables

## Product Fields

- Product ID
- Product name
- Category
- Brand and model
- Specification
- Compatible equipment
- Supplier
- Minimum stock
- Reorder quantity
- Lead time
- Storage location
- Expiry date where applicable
- Safety data sheet
- Status
