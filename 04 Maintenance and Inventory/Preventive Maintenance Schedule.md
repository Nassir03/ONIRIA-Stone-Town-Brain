# Preventive Maintenance Schedule

## Purpose

This schedule prevents avoidable failures in guest rooms, public areas, rooftop, spa, and building systems.

## Schedule Structure

| Frequency | Asset Types | Example Checks | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily | Pools, public areas, safety-critical visible items | Condition, cleanliness, obvious fault | Duty team |
| Weekly | Locks, AC visible performance, Wi-Fi reports | Guest-impact review | Engineering |
| Monthly | HVAC filters, KNX scenes, sensors, lighting | Function and condition | Engineering |
| Quarterly | Pool plant, pumps, water systems, fire equipment | Service and compliance evidence | Engineering / supplier |
| Semiannual | Major MEP systems | Deeper inspection | Engineering / specialist |
| Annual | Compliance systems, warranties, service contracts | Certification and renewal | Operations / compliance |

## Required Fields

- Schedule ID
- Equipment
- Room or area
- Frequency
- Task summary
- Responsible role
- Supplier if external
- Required evidence
- Next due date
- Last completed date
- Status
- Guest impact if overdue
