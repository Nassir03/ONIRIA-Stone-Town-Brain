# Duty Management Structure

## Every shift must identify

- Duty Manager
- Front Office/Guest Relations lead
- Housekeeping lead
- Engineering on-call contact
- Security contact
- Spa/F&B contacts when operating
- Incident Commander and deputies under the approved plan

## Handover minimum

- Occupancy, arrivals and departures
- VIP, accessibility, allergy and service-recovery needs with minimum necessary detail
- Rooms out of order and high-impact defects
- Open incidents, complaints and safety controls
- Supplier/delivery or stock risks affecting service
- Decisions required and deadlines
