# Performance Measures

## Balanced measures

- Guest request promise kept
- Complaint recovery and repeat-failure reduction
- Room readiness and inspection pass rate
- Preventive maintenance completed on time
- Critical defects and downtime
- Safety observations, near misses and corrective closure
- Training competence and renewal status
- Privacy incidents and access exceptions
- Inventory accuracy and stockouts
- Supplier quality and on-time delivery
- Staff wellbeing, attendance and retention

## Rule

Measures support learning and accountability. Never reward speed that causes unsafe work, hidden defects, privacy loss or poor guest treatment.
