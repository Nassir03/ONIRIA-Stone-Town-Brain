# Staff Onboarding Programme

## Before first shift

- Identity, contract and required employment records verified through approved process.
- Role, reporting line, duty schedule and authority explained.
- Confidentiality, guest privacy and acceptable system use completed.
- Emergency routes, alarms, assembly and reporting introduced.
- Uniform, grooming, access credentials and equipment issued and recorded.

## Core orientation

1. ONIRIA concept: heritage, intimate luxury and invisible technology.
2. Guest journey and service standards.
3. Lulu: what it does, disclosure as AI, when staff must verify or override.
4. Privacy, room access and information sharing.
5. Safety, security, fire, food/pool awareness and incident reporting.
6. Department SOPs, checklists and handover.
7. Practical supervised shifts.
8. Competence check and authorization before independent work.
