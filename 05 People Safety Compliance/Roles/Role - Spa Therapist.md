# Role - Spa Therapist

## Role purpose

Deliver approved treatments with consent, hygiene and privacy.

## Core responsibilities

- Follow approved SOPs and role authority.
- Protect guest, staff and business information.
- Record actions, handovers, incidents and unresolved work.
- Stop and escalate unsafe or unauthorized activity.
- Maintain required competence and training evidence.
- Cooperate with Lulu while independently verifying safety-critical decisions.
