# Privacy Incident Response

## Immediate actions

1. Stop ongoing exposure when safe and authorized.
2. Preserve logs, messages, access records and affected data.
3. Notify the Privacy Owner and Duty Manager immediately.
4. Record what happened, when, systems/people affected and current containment.
5. Do not delete evidence, blame individuals or contact affected people without approved coordination.
6. Assess risk with competent legal/privacy support.
7. Complete required notification and guest support under the approved plan.
8. Correct access, process or system failures and verify closure.

Final notification duties and timelines are TO CONFIRM through current legal review.
