# Pool Safety Framework

## Scope

Rooftop infinity pool, private in-room pools and terrace pools.

## Required controls

- Design/structural and safe-access approval
- Barriers, depth/signage and slip resistance
- Water treatment, testing and records
- Rescue and first-aid equipment
- Supervision/operating rules
- Glass, alcohol, weather and night-use controls
- Child and vulnerable-guest safeguards
- Plant isolation and maintenance
- Incident response and reopening authority
