# Food Safety Framework

## Required controls

- Approved food-safety responsibility between ONIRIA and SHI
- Supplier and receiving controls
- Temperature, storage, cleaning and pest controls
- Allergen information and cross-contact prevention
- Staff hygiene and illness reporting
- Traceability, complaints, incidents and corrective action
- Required inspections, permits and training evidence
