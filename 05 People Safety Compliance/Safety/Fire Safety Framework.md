# Fire Safety Framework

## Required controlled evidence

- Approved fire strategy and authority approvals
- Detection/alarm zones and cause-and-effect
- Evacuation routes, signage, emergency lighting and assembly point
- Extinguisher/hose/suppression layout as applicable
- Fire doors, compartmentation and penetrations
- Guest/staff accounting method
- Accessibility evacuation arrangements
- Inspection, testing and maintenance schedule
- Staff training and drill records
