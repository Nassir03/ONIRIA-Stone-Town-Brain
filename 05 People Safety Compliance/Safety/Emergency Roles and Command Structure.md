# Emergency Roles and Command Structure

## Command roles

- **Incident Commander:** Directs the hotel response until emergency services/authorized authority takes control.
- **Communications lead:** Calls responders, maintains verified internal messages and logs times.
- **Floor/zone wardens:** Guide evacuation and report zone status only where safe.
- **Guest-accounting lead:** Uses approved occupancy information to support accounting at assembly.
- **First-aid responders:** Provide care within current competence.
- **Engineering isolation lead:** Isolates utilities/equipment only when trained and safe.
- **Security/access lead:** Opens responder access and controls unsafe entry.
- **Guest-care lead:** Supports displaced guests, accessibility needs and privacy.

## Rules

- Life safety comes first.
- Never invent emergency instructions; use the approved site plan.
- Lulu supports alerts, records and coordination but a competent human leads physical emergency response.
- Final names, deputies, assembly points, routes, contacts and authority requirements are TO CONFIRM.
