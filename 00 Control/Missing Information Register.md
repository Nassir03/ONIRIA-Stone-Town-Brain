# Missing Information Register

## Purpose

This register prevents the Brain from silently guessing. Each gap must have an owner, evidence needed, affected notes, due date and escalation route.

| ID | Missing information | Affects | Priority | Owner | Evidence needed | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| MI-001 | Approved room-by-room dimensions and latest revisions | Rooms 01-20 | Critical | Design coordination | Current approved plans | Open |
| MI-002 | Coordinated RCP and ceiling access | Lighting, speakers, sensors, HVAC | Critical | MEP lead | Approved coordinated RCP | Open |
| MI-003 | HVAC equipment and controller basis | Room controls and maintenance | Critical | MEP lead | Equipment schedule and control narrative | Open |
| MI-004 | KNX architecture and point list | All smart rooms and public areas | Critical | Technology/MEP | Approved KNX design | Open |
| MI-005 | Room-by-room closure schedule | Pre-wire decisions | Critical | Site team | Current programme | Open |
| MI-006 | Final equipment schedules and model numbers | Asset register | High | Procurement/Engineering | Approved submittals and POs | Open |
| MI-007 | Approved operating systems: PMS, channel manager, booking engine | Front Office SOPs | High | Operations/Lulu | Signed selection decision | Open |
| MI-008 | Approved staffing plan and employment framework | Staffing and training | High | CEO/HR | Approved organization and legal review | Open |
| MI-009 | Approved fire, food, pool, privacy and security requirements | Safety/compliance | Critical | Compliance owner | Authority/consultant evidence | Open |
| MI-010 | Supplier terms, warranties and Zanzibar delivery details | Supplier records | High | Procurement | Signed contracts/quotes | Open |
| MI-011 | Room 15 render reference or confirmation no render exists | Room 15 / visual room evidence | Medium | Design coordination | Current room render folder or approved design issue | Open |

## Lulu action

Use [[Lulu Missing Information Alerts]] to alert the responsible owner when a critical gap blocks construction, procurement, opening readiness, safety or guest service.
