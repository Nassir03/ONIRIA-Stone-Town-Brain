# SOP - Room Service and SHI Coordination

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Food and Beverage]] and [[Department - Guest Relations]]
- **Guest journey stage:** In-stay
- **Request/incident type:** Food and beverage request
- **Related room/area:** Guest room/SHI
- **Equipment/system:** POS/PMS/chat
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** F&B Manager
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Receive the order and confirm room, items, timing, dietary needs and price.
2. Check availability with SHI; offer only confirmed alternatives.
3. Enter the order in the approved system and assign delivery ownership.
4. Apply [[SOP - Food Allergy Handling]] when any allergy is declared.
5. Prepare and verify order accuracy, temperature, presentation and charge routing.
6. Notify the guest of delivery timing and any delay before the promise expires.
7. Deliver with privacy and room-entry protocol.
8. Confirm placement and obtain required signature/authorization.
9. Arrange collection of service items.
10. Record completion and service issues.

## Records created

- Task/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert
