# SOP - Guest Data Privacy

## Control

- **Department:** All departments
- **Guest journey stage:** All
- **Request/incident type:** Privacy request/incident
- **Related room/area:** All systems and areas
- **Equipment/system:** Approved systems only
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Privacy Owner
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Collect only information needed for service, safety, payment or legal duties.
2. Explain why preference data is requested and allow the guest to decline optional personalization.
3. Store guest data only in approved systems with role-based access.
4. Never place sensitive details in public notes, open chat channels or room labels.
5. Verify identity before disclosing, changing or deleting guest information.
6. Share with staff only on a need-to-know basis.
7. Do not expose one guest’s data to another guest, supplier or unauthorized person.
8. Report suspected loss, misdirection or unauthorized access immediately.
9. Preserve evidence and follow [[Privacy Incident Response]].
10. Apply approved retention and deletion rules.

## Records created

- Task/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert

## Lulu workflow

Lulu retrieves this SOP, checks permission and current facts, assigns the responsible role, tracks the deadline, protects private data, records the result and escalates exceptions through [[Lulu Permissions and Escalation]].

## Review triggers

- System or supplier change
- New legal/safety requirement
- Incident or complaint trend
- Changed staffing/authority
- Failed test or audit
