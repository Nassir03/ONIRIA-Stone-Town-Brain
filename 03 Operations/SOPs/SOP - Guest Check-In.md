# SOP - Guest Check-In

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Front Office]]
- **Guest journey stage:** Arrival
- **Request/incident type:** Guest arrival/check-in
- **Related room/area:** Reception and assigned room
- **Equipment/system:** PMS, payment terminal, smart lock
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Duty Manager
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Review the reservation, guest profile, preferences, transfer and room-readiness status before arrival.
2. Confirm the room has passed inspection and personalized settings are applied.
3. Welcome the guest warmly; disclose Lulu as an AI-operated service when relevant and offer human support.
4. Verify identity and reservation details discreetly; collect only required information.
5. Complete registration, payment authorization and required records in the approved PMS.
6. Confirm allergies, urgent preferences and communication channel without repeating information already provided.
7. Issue and test the room credential; never announce the room number publicly.
8. Explain essential services, privacy controls and how to contact Lulu/the team.
9. Escort or arrange escort, introduce the room simply, and verify first comfort settings.
10. Record completion, unresolved requests and any follow-up owner.

## Records created

- Action/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert

## Lulu workflow

Lulu retrieves this SOP, checks permission and current facts, assigns the responsible role, tracks the deadline, protects private data, records the result and escalates exceptions through [[Lulu Permissions and Escalation]].

## Review triggers

- System or supplier change
- New legal/safety requirement
- Incident or complaint trend
- Changed staffing/authority
- Failed test or audit
