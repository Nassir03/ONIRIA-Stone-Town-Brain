# SOP - Food Allergy Handling

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Food and Beverage]]
- **Guest journey stage:** Pre-arrival/In-stay
- **Request/incident type:** Food allergy
- **Related room/area:** SHI/hotel service points
- **Equipment/system:** POS/PMS/allergen records
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** F&B Manager
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Treat every declared allergy as a safety-critical request.
2. Record the allergen, severity statement and guest identity privately in approved systems.
3. Do not promise an allergen-free meal unless the responsible F&B lead confirms controls.
4. Communicate the request directly to the kitchen using the approved allergen process.
5. Verify ingredients, preparation area, utensils, cross-contact controls and final plate.
6. Mark and hand over the meal using a positive identification method.
7. Confirm with the guest before serving.
8. For symptoms, stop service and begin emergency response; do not diagnose or delay escalation.
9. Record the incident or near miss.
10. Review any failure before further service.
