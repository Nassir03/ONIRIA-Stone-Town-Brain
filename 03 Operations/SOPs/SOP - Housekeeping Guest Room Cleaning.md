# SOP - Housekeeping Guest Room Cleaning

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Housekeeping]]
- **Guest journey stage:** In-stay/Post-stay
- **Request/incident type:** Cleaning
- **Related room/area:** Guest rooms
- **Equipment/system:** Housekeeping tools/PPE
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Housekeeping Supervisor
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Confirm room status, access permission, privacy signs and special instructions.
2. Announce presence and enter only under approved access rules.
3. Ventilate and perform a safety/maintenance scan before cleaning.
4. Remove waste and used linen using contamination and lost-item controls.
5. Clean from high to low and clean to dirty; use the approved product for each surface.
6. Sanitize high-touch points and bathroom fixtures; prevent cross-contamination.
7. Replace linen, towels and amenities according to stay status and guest preference.
8. Reset furniture, minibar, lighting, climate and curtains to the approved room standard.
9. Report defects, damage, unusual items or safety concerns without disturbing evidence.
10. Complete final inspection, secure the room and update status.

- Action/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
