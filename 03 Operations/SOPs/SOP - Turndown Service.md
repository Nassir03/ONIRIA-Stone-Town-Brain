# SOP - Turndown Service

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Housekeeping]]
- **Guest journey stage:** In-stay
- **Request/incident type:** Turndown
- **Related room/area:** Guest rooms
- **Equipment/system:** Housekeeping tools/KNX
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Housekeeping Supervisor
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Confirm guest preference, timing, privacy status and room occupancy.
2. Enter under approved access rules.
3. Refresh the room lightly; remove visible waste and replace used towels as required.
4. Prepare the bed and bedside area.
5. Set evening lighting, curtains, temperature and music only to approved preferences.
6. Replenish water and approved amenities.
7. Check bathroom presentation and safety.
8. Do not move or inspect guest possessions beyond what service requires.
9. Record maintenance or minibar issues.
10. Secure the room and update completion.

## Records created

- Action/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert
