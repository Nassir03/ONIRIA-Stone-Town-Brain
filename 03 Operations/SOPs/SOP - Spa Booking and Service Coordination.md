# SOP - Spa Booking and Service Coordination

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Spa]]
- **Guest journey stage:** Pre-arrival/In-stay
- **Request/incident type:** Spa booking
- **Related room/area:** Spa
- **Equipment/system:** Booking/PMS/chat
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Spa Manager
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Confirm requested treatment, preferred time, number of guests and communication channel.
2. Check therapist, room and equipment availability.
3. Provide accurate duration, price, preparation and cancellation terms.
4. Collect only required health/safety information privately; do not diagnose.
5. Flag contraindications for qualified human review.
6. Confirm booking in the approved system and send a concise confirmation.
7. Prepare the treatment space and guest preferences.
8. Verify identity and consent before service.
9. Record completion, payment and relevant non-sensitive preferences.
10. Escalate adverse reactions or safety concerns immediately.

## Records created

- Action/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert
