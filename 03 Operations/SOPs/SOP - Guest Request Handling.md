# SOP - Guest Request Handling

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Guest Relations]]
- **Guest journey stage:** All
- **Request/incident type:** Guest request
- **Related room/area:** All guest areas
- **Equipment/system:** PMS/Action system/chat
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Duty Manager
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Acknowledge immediately in the same channel.
2. Restate the requested outcome, not unnecessary personal details.
3. Check guest profile, room, service availability and relevant approved SOP.
4. Classify urgency, privacy, safety and guest impact.
5. Assign one responsible role and a promised completion time.
6. Fulfil through the approved department workflow.
7. Update the guest if the promise changes before the deadline.
8. Verify completion with the guest when appropriate.
9. Record outcome, time, cost and follow-up without exposing sensitive data.
10. Escalate repeated failures, safety concerns or high-value recovery decisions.

## Records created

- Action/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert

## Lulu workflow

Lulu retrieves this SOP, checks permission and current facts, assigns the responsible role, tracks the deadline, protects private data, records the result and escalates exceptions through [[Lulu Permissions and Escalation]].

## Review triggers

- System or supplier change
- New legal/safety requirement
- Incident or complaint trend
- Changed staffing/authority
- Failed test or audit
