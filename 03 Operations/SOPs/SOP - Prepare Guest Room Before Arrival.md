# SOP - Prepare Guest Room Before Arrival

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Housekeeping]] and [[Department - Guest Relations]]
- **Guest journey stage:** Pre-arrival
- **Request/incident type:** Room preparation
- **Related room/area:** Assigned guest room
- **Equipment/system:** PMS, KNX, housekeeping tools
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Housekeeping Supervisor
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Confirm room assignment, arrival time, occupancy and approved preferences.
2. Ensure construction/maintenance defects are closed and room is released.
3. Complete cleaning and supervisor inspection.
4. Place correct linen, amenities, minibar and any approved special setup.
5. Apply temperature, lighting, music, scent, pillow and curtain preferences only where consented and technically available.
6. Verify allergies and remove conflicting products or food items.
7. Test smart lock, Wi-Fi, voice/audio, lighting scenes, HVAC and essential fixtures.
8. Record any unavailable preference and prepare a truthful alternative.
9. Mark room ready only after all critical checks pass.
10. Notify Front Office/Lulu of readiness and unresolved non-critical items.

## Records created

- Action/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert
