# SOP - Lost and Found

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Security]] and [[Department - Housekeeping]]
- **Guest journey stage:** Post-stay/In-stay
- **Request/incident type:** Lost item
- **Related room/area:** All areas
- **Equipment/system:** Lost-and-found register
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Duty Manager
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Receive the report and record description, location, time and contact channel.
2. Search only authorized areas and preserve guest privacy.
3. When found, use two-person verification for valuables and record finder, time and condition.
4. Secure the item in the designated controlled storage.
5. Verify ownership using details not publicly disclosed.
6. Agree collection or shipment in writing; do not expose the item or guest identity.
7. Record release, recipient, delivery evidence and any cost.
8. Escalate cash, passports, controlled items, suspected crime or hazardous items.
9. Retain and dispose only under the approved retention rule.
10. Audit open items regularly.

## Records created

- Action/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert
