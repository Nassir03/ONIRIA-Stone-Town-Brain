# SOP - Guest Check-Out

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Front Office]]
- **Guest journey stage:** Departure
- **Request/incident type:** Check-out
- **Related room/area:** Reception/guest chat
- **Equipment/system:** PMS, payment terminal
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Duty Manager
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Review folio, open requests, transport and departure preferences before contacting the guest.
2. Confirm all charges from hotel, spa and SHI interfaces are accurate and supported.
3. Present the folio through the guest’s preferred private channel.
4. Resolve questions before payment; escalate disputed charges to the Duty Manager.
5. Complete payment, issue receipt and close the stay in the PMS.
6. Confirm luggage, transfer and late-departure arrangements.
7. Ask one focused feedback question; do not pressure the guest for a review.
8. Record approved preferences and service-recovery history with privacy controls.
9. Trigger room status change and housekeeping inspection.
10. Send post-stay thanks, feedback and review request at the approved time.

## Records created

- Task/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert
