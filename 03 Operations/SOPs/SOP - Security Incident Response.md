# SOP - Security Incident Response

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Security]] and all departments
- **Guest journey stage:** All
- **Request/incident type:** Security incident
- **Related room/area:** All areas
- **Equipment/system:** Access control/CCTV if approved
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Duty Manager
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Protect people and create distance from immediate danger.
2. Alert security/Duty Manager and emergency services as required.
3. Do not confront or detain beyond training and lawful authority.
4. Preserve guest privacy and avoid public discussion.
5. Control access to the affected area.
6. Preserve evidence, logs and recordings without alteration.
7. Record objective observations, not assumptions.
8. Support affected guests and arrange service recovery after safety is stable.
9. Escalate threats, violence, missing persons, suspected crime or data/security overlap.
10. Complete incident review and corrective action.

## Records created

- Task/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert

## Lulu workflow

Lulu retrieves this SOP, checks permission and current facts, assigns the responsible role, tracks the deadline, protects private data, records the result and escalates exceptions through [[Lulu Permissions and Escalation]].

## Review triggers

- System or supplier change
- New legal/safety requirement
- Incident or complaint trend
- Changed staffing/authority
- Failed test or audit
