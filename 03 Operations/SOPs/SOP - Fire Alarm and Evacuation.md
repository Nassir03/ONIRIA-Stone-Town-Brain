# SOP - Fire Alarm and Evacuation

## Control

- **Department:** All departments
- **Guest journey stage:** All
- **Request/incident type:** Fire/alarm
- **Related room/area:** All areas
- **Equipment/system:** Alarm, extinguishers, evacuation aids
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Incident Commander
- **Approved REFERENCE:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. On discovering smoke/fire, raise the alarm and report exact location.
2. Call the approved emergency service/contact without delay.
3. Evacuate using the approved route; never use unsafe routes or re-enter.
4. Close doors when safe; do not lock them.
5. Use firefighting equipment only if trained, the fire is small and escape remains clear.
6. Assist guests, including those needing mobility or communication support.
7. Sweep only assigned zones when safe and report status.
8. Assemble and account for people at the approved point.
9. Follow emergency-responder instructions.
10. Do not reset systems or reopen until authorized.
