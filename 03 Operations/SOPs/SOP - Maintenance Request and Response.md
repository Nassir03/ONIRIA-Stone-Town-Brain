# SOP - Maintenance Request and Response

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Engineering]]
- **Guest journey stage:** All
- **Request/incident type:** Maintenance request
- **Related room/area:** Rooms and areas
- **Equipment/system:** CMMS/task system/tools
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Chief Engineer
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Record exact location, symptom, time, reporter and guest impact.
2. Classify as emergency, urgent, routine or planned.
3. For immediate danger, isolate the area/equipment and start emergency escalation.
4. Check asset history, warranty, manual and approved isolation procedure.
5. Assign a competent person with deadline and required parts.
6. Inform Front Office before entering an occupied room and follow privacy/access rules.
7. Diagnose and repair without bypassing safety controls.
8. Test operation, restore the area and clean all work traces.
9. Confirm guest recovery action when service was affected.
10. Record cause, labor, parts, downtime, evidence and follow-up.

## Records created

- Task/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert

Lulu retrieves this SOP, checks permission and current facts, assigns the responsible role, tracks the deadline, protects private data, records the result and escalates exceptions through [[Lulu Permissions and Escalation]].

- System or supplier change
- New legal/safety requirement
- Incident or complaint trend
- Changed staffing/authority
- Failed test or audit
