# SOP - Complaint and Service Recovery

## Control

- **Department:** [[Department - Guest Relations]]
- **Guest journey stage:** All
- **Request/incident type:** Complaint
- **Related room/area:** All guest areas
- **Equipment/system:** Case/task record
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** General Manager
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Listen without interruption and acknowledge the impact.
2. Apologize for the experience without arguing or blaming.
3. Protect privacy; move the conversation away from public areas when needed.
4. Establish the facts, desired outcome and any immediate safety need.
5. Correct the immediate problem or assign the responsible team with a clear deadline.
6. Select recovery within role authority, considering guest impact, fairness and cost.
7. Escalate serious injury, discrimination, privacy, security, legal, media or major financial cases.
8. Confirm the guest accepts the solution and schedule follow-up.
9. Record root cause, actions, recovery and evidence.
10. Close only after follow-up and preventive action are assigned.

## Records created

- Task/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert

## Lulu workflow

Lulu retrieves this SOP, checks permission and current facts, assigns the responsible role, tracks the deadline, protects private data, records the result and escalates exceptions through [[Lulu Permissions and Escalation]].

## Review triggers

- System or supplier change
- New legal/safety requirement
- Incident or complaint trend
- Changed staffing/authority
- Failed test or audit
