# SOP - Pool Incident Response

## Control

- **Department:** Rooftop/pool operations
- **Guest journey stage:** In-stay
- **Request/incident type:** Pool incident
- **Related room/area:** Rooftop pool/room pools
- **Equipment/system:** Rescue and first-aid equipment
- **Responsible role:** TO CONFIRM in staffing plan
- **Escalation:** Duty Manager
- **Approved source:** Pre-opening operational design; requires formal approval and system-specific validation.

## Standard


1. Raise alarm and stop further access to the hazard.
2. Initiate rescue only within training and without creating another victim.
3. Call emergency support and provide exact location/access route.
4. Provide first aid within competence and continue until qualified help takes over.
5. Control bystanders and protect guest dignity.
6. Isolate equipment/water system when relevant.
7. Preserve evidence and obtain witness details.
8. Notify management and required responsible parties.
9. Record water conditions, equipment status and actions.
10. Reopen only after competent inspection and authorization.

## Records created

- Task/request/incident record as applicable
- Time, owner, completion and evidence
- Guest-impact and recovery record where applicable
- Corrective action or missing-information alert
