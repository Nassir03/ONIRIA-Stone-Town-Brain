# Checklist - Daily Operations

## Morning
- Review occupancy, arrivals, departures, preferences, room status and open incidents.
- Review critical maintenance, safety, staffing, stock and supplier risks.
- Confirm rooftop/pool/spa/F&B readiness.

## Midday
- Check overdue guest requests and service promises.
- Confirm room readiness and engineering response.
- Review stockouts, delivery changes and safety observations.

## Evening
- Confirm arrivals and turndown status.
- Review unresolved complaints and high-impact defects.
- Issue concise handover with owner and next action.
