# Checklist - Department Opening

- [ ] Area safe, clean and accessible.
- [ ] Staff attendance and duty roles confirmed.
- [ ] Handover and unresolved incidents reviewed.
- [ ] Systems, equipment and communications tested.
- [ ] Guest arrivals, preferences, allergies and VIP/service-recovery notes reviewed privately.
- [ ] Stock and critical supplies checked.
- [ ] Cash/payment controls completed where applicable.
- [ ] Safety equipment visually checked.
- [ ] Maintenance defects reported and impact controlled.
- [ ] Department marked ready by responsible supervisor.
