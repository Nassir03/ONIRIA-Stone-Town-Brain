# Checklist - Department Closing

- [ ] Guest requests completed or handed over with owner and deadline.
- [ ] Incidents, complaints and maintenance issues recorded.
- [ ] Cash/payment and keys/access controls reconciled where applicable.
- [ ] Sensitive information secured.
- [ ] Equipment shut down or left in approved operating mode.
- [ ] Stock variances and reorder needs recorded.
- [ ] Area cleaned, locked and fire routes kept clear.
- [ ] Next shift brief prepared.
- [ ] Critical issues escalated before departure.
