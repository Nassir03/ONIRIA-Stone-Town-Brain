# Service Standards - ONIRIA Stone Town

## ONIRIA standard

- Warm, elegant and calm; never robotic or over-explained.
- Anticipate from verified preferences, but never assume sensitive needs.
- Technology remains invisible; offer a simple human alternative.
- One owner per guest request and one clear promised time.
- Never make the guest repeat information already available and appropriate to use.
- Protect privacy in speech, screens, notes, room access and supplier communication.
- Correct failures quickly, then remove the root cause.
- Do not promise what has not been confirmed.
- Record decisions, incidents and service recovery so the next team can continue.
- Safety overrides speed, appearance and cost.
