# Room Category - Master Suite

## Standard

- **Number of rooms:** 3
- **Rooms:** Rooms 14, 19, 20
- **Confirmed concept:** Terrace, jacuzzi/bathtub and private above-ground glass-fibre terrace pool, subject to current approved design.

## Category-level standards to approve

- Space and occupancy standard
- Bed configuration
- Bathroom standard
- Furniture and minibar standard
- Lighting scenes
- HVAC comfort range
- Audio and scent capability
- Smart-lock and privacy controls
- Cleaning and inspection standard
- Guest amenities

## Control

Room-specific approved drawings override this category note. Differences must be recorded in the relevant room note.
