# Room Inspection Standard

## Inspection gates

1. Design information approved.
2. First-fix location verified before closure.
3. Second-fix installation checked.
4. Systems commissioned and evidence attached.
5. Furniture and equipment installed without damage.
6. Housekeeping clean completed.
7. Safety and privacy checks passed.
8. Guest-experience scene tested.
9. Snags assigned with owners and due dates.
10. Room released only after all critical defects are closed.

## Required test areas

- Door, smart lock and privacy indicators
- Lighting fixtures and all KNX scenes
- HVAC operation and noise
- Water pressure, hot water, drainage and leaks
- Wi-Fi coverage and guest network
- Speakers and voice interaction
- Sensors and automation behavior
- Curtains/blinds where applicable
- Minibar, sockets, charging and appliances
- Bathroom fixtures, seals and finishes
- Fire/life-safety interfaces
- Cleanliness, linen, amenities and scent
